"Cycle the Continental Divide"

Tour Overview

Our Cycle the Continental Divide in two weeks will capture the beauty of the mountains and the colors and character of the Tour as it passes through the Appalachian Mountains. Combining peaceful yet challenging ride days with the spectacle of the Continent's biggest race, whilst enjoying every minute with The Mountain Spoke. Riding and watching the Tour in the same week is a surreal experience that often has us pinching ourselves in disbelief at just how magical the mountains can be.

Dates: June 12-28
Route: begins in Banff, Alberta, Canada to Antelope Wells, New Mexico, USA
Cost: $2,000
Basic Requirements: bikepack, bicycle, tent, food
